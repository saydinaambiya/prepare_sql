Query the list of CITY names from STATION that do not start with vowels and do not end with vowels. Your result cannot contain duplicates.
SQL :
SELECT DISTINCT CITY FROM STATION WHERE (CITY NOT LIKE 'A%' AND CITY NOT LIKE 'I%' AND CITY NOT LIKE 'U%' AND CITY NOT LIKE 'E%' AND CITY NOT LIKE 'O%') AND (CITY NOT LIKE '%a' AND CITY NOT LIKE '%i' AND CITY NOT LIKE '%u' AND CITY NOT LIKE '%e' AND CITY NOT LIKE '%o') ORDER BY CITY;
